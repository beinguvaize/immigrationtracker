Welcome Applicants

A Message from the Creator

Dear PNP and AIP applicants,

My name is Uvaize, and I understand how challenging and uncertain the immigration process can be especially with the current processing delays in provinces such as Nova Scotia. Waiting for updates, monitoring status changes, and trying to plan your future around shifting timelines can be stressful both financially and emotionally.

This tracker has been created with a clear purpose: to help us plan, monitor, and navigate our immigration journeys together through shared and updated information.

For applicants under programs like the Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) and the Atlantic Immigration Program (AIP), processing times can vary depending on the stream, province, and application stage. Official timelines often provide general estimates, but real applicant data helps us understand actual trends, average stage movements, and realistic expectations.

The tracker is designed to:

  • ✓ Monitor application stages collectively
  • ✓ Identify processing trends
  • ✓ Help with better financial and career planning
  • ✓ Reduce uncertainty through shared information
  • ✓ Build a supportive applicant community

Immigration is not just about documentation, it impacts careers, families, and long-term settlement plans. By sharing timelines and updates transparently, we create a practical reference that benefits everyone.

If anyone needs additional assistance with the tracker, has suggestions for improvement, or faces any technical difficulties, please feel free to contact me directly at uvaize@hotmail.com. I will do my best to support you and ensure the tracker remains helpful and accurate for everyone.

Together, we move forward step by step toward permanent residency and stability in Canada.
